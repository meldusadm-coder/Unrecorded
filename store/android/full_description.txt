Unrecorded helps you understand possible privacy risk from nearby Bluetooth signals that may match smart glasses or wearable recording devices.

What it does:
- Scans for nearby BLE signals on your device
- Compares signals against patterns associated with known smart glasses and wearables
- Shows a privacy-risk level (low / medium / high) with plain-English explanations

What it does not do:
- Prove that anyone is recording
- Detect non-Bluetooth cameras or microphones
- Upload scan data off your device by default

Bluetooth signals can be hidden, randomised, or spoofed. Unrecorded provides risk indicators — not certainty. Treat alerts as awareness signals only.

Scan data stays on your device. No account required.
