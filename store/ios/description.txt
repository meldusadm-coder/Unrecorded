Unrecorded helps you understand possible privacy risk from nearby Bluetooth signals that may match smart glasses or wearable recording devices.

Features:
- On-device BLE scanning
- Risk levels with plain-English reasons
- Local-first: scan data stays on your device

Limitations:
- Cannot prove that anyone is recording
- May not detect devices with hidden or randomised names
- Bluetooth-only; does not detect all cameras or microphones

Treat results as awareness signals, not proof of recording.
